About Us


Mission
To support young athlets living in Chicago's northside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with oportunities to learn and practice skills relates to the game of soccer, specificallyt those skills araund team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision
The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, Well-rounded and mindful athlets through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General Information
The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athlets residing in Chicago's northside neighborhoods on enviroment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you mast be between the ages of 4-12 and resides in a Chicago's northside neighborhood NYSL is ran by a small full-time stoff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
